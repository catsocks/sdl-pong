Tennis

Tennis is a clone of the Pong arcade game that you can play in your browser with a keyboard, gamepads and touch.

Controls

Keyboard

WS moves the left paddle up and down
↑↓ moves the right paddle up and down
R restarts the round
M toggles sound
P toggles pause
F11 toggles fullscreen

Gamepad

moves a paddle up and down

Touch

  • Tap either side of the screen to move a paddle.
  • Double tap on the middle of the screen to toggle fullscreen.

You should switch to landscape mode.
